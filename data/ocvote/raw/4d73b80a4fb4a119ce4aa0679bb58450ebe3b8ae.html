Voting Options

Information on voting options, how to return your ballot, and Vote Centers, go to the Voting section of our website. Take advantage of the alternatives available to avoid congestion – from voting at home, drive-thru ballot drop-off, in-person options, returning your ballot via the United States Postal Service, or personally deliver your ballot to us – the choice is yours.
