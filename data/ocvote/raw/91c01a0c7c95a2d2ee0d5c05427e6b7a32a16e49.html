Remote Accessible Ballots for Eligible Voters

Vote independently. Vote privately.

The Remote Accessible Vote-By-Mail ballot provides voters with disabilities and overseas voters the option to request a Vote-By-Mail ballot to be delivered electronically. This will also be available to all voters for the 2022 Statewide Direct Primary Election. The electronic ballot can be downloaded to the voter’s computer, marked using the voter’s own assistive technology and then printed.

To start, click on the button below and look up your voter information. From there, click on View a fully accessible Voter Information Guide and select Accessible Vote by Mail Ballot on the page that loads.
