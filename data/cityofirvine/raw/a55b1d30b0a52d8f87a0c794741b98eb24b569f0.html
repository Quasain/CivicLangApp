Health Care Resources

If you need assistance in health care, please refer to the following resources:

  • Be Well OC
    • Be Well OC is working to make Orange County the happiest and healthiest community in the United States—and that starts with a world-class mental health care system. The mission is to make compassionate mental health care more accessible for our community.​
    • ​ 949-749-2500
    • Be Well Irvine - Mobile Unit
  • Irvine Children's Health Program (ICHP)
    • The Irvine Children's Health Program is a partnership between the City of Irvine and the Coalition of Orange County Community Health Centers. Certified Enrollment Counselors from the Coalition help families and individuals enroll in health insurance programs. Assistance is available at no cost.
    • 949-724-6690
  • KCS Healthcenter
    • Provide client-centered, culturally inclusive, expert care to directly improve the well-being of underserved communities and individuals through healthcare, social services, and community programs.
    • 714-503-6550
    • 19742 MacArthur Blvd. Ste 250, Irvine, CA 92612
    • Monday – Friday: 9 a.m.-5 p.m.
    • Languages: English, Korean, Chinese
  • Medi-Cal
    • Medi-Cal is a health care program that pays for a variety of medical services for children and adults with limited income and resources.
FOR Families Appointments

For more information or to schedule an appointment, email forfamilies@cityofirvine.org or call 949-724-6650.

FOR Families is a confidential, free service to those who live in Irvine (some legal exceptions may apply.)

FOR Families Brochure

24-Hour Crisis Line

National Suicide Prevention & Crisis Lifeline
9-8-8

FOR Families is open during regular business hours only. For 24-hour resources and support, please call the countywide help line, 211.
